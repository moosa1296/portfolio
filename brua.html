About Project Detail

The purpose of the Norwegian National Coordination Centre for research and innovation on cybersecurity (NCC-NO) is to increase research, innovation, and technological developments within cybersecurity in Norway. NCC-NO promotes national and international cooperation on research, innovation, and technology in cybersecurity. The EU extends support and investments in the wealth of experience in cybersecurity research, technological, and industrial developments within the Union by prioritizing cybersecurity through Horizon Europe and the DIGITAL Europe program. Furthermore, the EU is pooling resources and expertise by establishing the European Cybersecurity Competence Centre (ECCC) and the network of national coordination centres (NCCs).
