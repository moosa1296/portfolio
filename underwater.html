About Project Detail

We used two different deep learning models to perform classification. Both models, ResNet18 and MobileNetV2 were initialized with pre-trained weights to leverage transfer learning, enhancing their capability to extract relevant features from mel-spectrograms with minimal training on the target dataset. The final layer of each model was adjusted to match the number of classes in the underwater acoustic dataset. Training involved optimizing the cross-entropy loss function using the Adam optimizer, with learning rate adjustments managed by a StepLR scheduler to reduce the learning rate at predetermined epochs, facilitating better convergence.
